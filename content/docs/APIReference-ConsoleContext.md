---
title: ConsoleContext
date: "2018-03-22"
---

Extends from [Context](https://bottender.js.org/docs/APIReference-Context).

#### `platform`

Example:

```js
context.platform; // 'console'
```

#### `sendText(text)`

Send text to the owner of the session.

Example:

```js
context.sendText('Hello');
```
